HDB Resale Prices Analysis and Prediction

This is my own project to analyze HDB resale prices in Singapore. In the notebook, I delve into factors that affect housing pricing such as location, flat type, number of lease years remaining, size of flat, and what storey the HDB resides in. At the end of the analysis, I created a predictive model to predict housing pricing and deployed the model into huggingface spaces.
