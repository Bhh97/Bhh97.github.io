Netflix Analysis and Recommendations

Using the Netflix Movies and TV Shows dataset found here, where the goal was to uncover insights into Netflix's content and trends over the years. Predicting Credit Defaulters

Using the Credit Risk dataset from Kaggle, the goal is to predict whether a customer will deafult on their loan. For this project, I used Apache Spark to predict loan defaults. Firstly, I explored various demographic and financial features to assess the probability of loan defaults. My approach integrates data preprocessing and feature engineering in Apache Spark, followed by training a logistic regression model to discern the subtle patterns that indicate risk. The project's cornerstone is the model's capability to accurately gauge creditworthiness, a key metric in the financial sector.
